The sports club program at The Train Club is comprised of sports club formed ,developed,governed under the authority of the club.The key to the succes if the club is the players and students leadership,intrests,involvement and participations.

Purpose of the sports club

The sports club program here offers the community an opportunity to participatein diffrent sports of diverse groups to achive common goal and objectives while encouraging healthy lifestyles. The club provides various learnings through student involvement in fundraising, public relations, organization, administration, budgeting, scheduling, etc. The most succesfull clubs are those whith the most number of active membersand dedicated officers.
